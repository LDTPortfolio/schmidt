S. 4.1. Collaborative Practice:

Group Project: Front-End Analysis

by Olivia Schmidt 24th September 2023 EME 6284

“Candidates collaborate with their peers and subject matter experts to analyze learners, develop and design instruction, and evaluate its impact on learners.”

This artifact was one of the first products of our group computer-based training (CBT) – our group Front-End Analysis (FEA). I collaborated with my three peers, who had also created individual FEAs, to combine our work as we felt was appropriate and bounce feedback off of one another to come to a coherent final FEA. In this initial document, we analyzed our potential learners, drafted our initial instruction plan, and evaluated our training’s potential impact on learners. Deliverables for this assignment included evaluating the context of the training; assessing the target audience; drafting design implications; forming a goal/task Analysis; and proposing methods and results.
