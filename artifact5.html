"Candidates implement appropriate educational technologies and processes based on appropriate content pedagogy."

In this assignment, I worked with three other classmates over the semester to design an online, interactive experience for our client, Innovation Education, and their contact with Dr. Eleni Henderson to develop an online alternative to the usual in-person course Introduction to Hospitality and Tourism Management. Our experience was designed keeping in mind that it would be an integral component of the online course and replace the traditional on-campus field trip experience. Our experience goes through 8 stops in Tampa, primarily downtown Tampa, that have a significant impact on the tourism/hospitality industry. While reimagining this activity for the digital environment, we included 360-degree interactions, AI-powered “guides” for each stop of the tour, interactive assessments, audio and video Content, and high-quality imagery. Our use of Articulate Storyline and other tools, such as HeyGen’s AI service, aligns with the use of educational technology to deliver appropriate content pedagogy.
