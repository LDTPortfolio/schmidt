S. 1.2 Using:

Web 2.0 Tool Review

by Olivia Schmidt 5th October 2022 EME 6055

"Candidates demonstrate the ability to select and use technological resources and processes to support student learning and to enhance their pedagogy."

My peers and I were assigned various emerging Web 2.0 tools to explore; after selecting the Web 2.0 tool we felt was the most user-friendly and beneficial for learning activity design, we were to craft a thorough review of its purpose, functionalities, and suggestions for using the application with students/trainees to enhance their learning and creativity. Critically, we were also supposed to identify potential barriers or problems that educators using this Web 2.0 tool might encounter. I discovered “Goosechase”, an application where teachers may create multimodal scavenger hunts - students compete to complete “missions,” that are based on photos/videos, or GPS. I wrote a thorough review of Goosechase, as well as produced a complementary video that goes into further depth explaining how to use the application.
