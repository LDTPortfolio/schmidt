S. 1.3. Assessing/Evaluating:

Educational Game Evaluation

by Olivia Schmidt 16th October 2022 EME 6055

"Candidates demonstrate the ability to assess and evaluate the effective integration of appropriate technologies and instructional materials"

Similar to the previous artifact, in this assignment, we were assigned various browser-based educational games to explore and critique. The development process of producing such a critique began with my selection of perhaps the most famous educational computer game ever created – The Oregon Trail. Using an evaluation criteria outlined in the assignment, I provided a review of both The Oregon Trail and another browser-based game I found by searching PBS Kid’s game repository. My critique directly aligns with the goal of demonstrating the ability to assess and evaluate the effective integration of appropriate technologies and instructional materials; I applied a structured evaluation criteria to analyze the educational value, user experience, challenge and support, and overall effectiveness of these two educational games.
