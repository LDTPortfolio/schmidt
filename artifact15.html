S. 5.4. Ethics:

EME 6347 Final Projects

by Olivia Schmidt 2nd May 2023 EME 6347

“Candidates conduct research and practice using accepted professional and institutional guidelines and procedures.”

The final assignment of Digital Media and Learning was created to be flexible to the desires of what students in this course thought would best benefit them professionally, personally, or academically. The form of this assignment was not static; one could choose to write a paper, create a piece of art, develop a website, conduct “legally sanctioned demolition” (sourced from the humor of the professor who was running the course), etc. I likely cannot lift a sledgehammer, so I opted to write a paper instead. You can read the result of this on my personal portfolio website, at the link below. As for the relation to the 5.3 AECT Standard; this paper is inherently concerned with ethics, and professional considerations – my paper touches on the socio-cultural, economic, and political aspects of online communities, discussing how they have been influenced by commercial platforms and corporate forces.
