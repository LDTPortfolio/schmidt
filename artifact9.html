S. 3.3. Assessing:

Final EME 6055 Paper & Presentation

by Olivia Schmidt 30th November 2022 EME 6055

“Candidates use multiple assessment strategies to collect data for informing decisions to improve instructional practice, learner outcomes, and the learning environment.”

This paper and accompanying video was the accumulating assessment of Current Trends in Instructional Technology. We were instructed to write a short essay paper on the vision of emerging technologies; our vision of "The Classroom of the Future." My paper explores the concept of Game-Based Learning (GBL) and its potential benefits in education, along with the need for teachers to have technological literacy and a pedagogical vision when using games in the classroom. I offer recommendations for successful GBL implementation, emphasizing the importance of clear pedagogical goals, customized learning experiences, feedback, and ongoing assessment. To complement my paper, I also created a voice-narrated PPT presentation that goes over my recommendations and the challenges teachers face in preparing students for the digital age.
