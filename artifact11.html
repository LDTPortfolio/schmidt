S. 4.2. Leadership:

Draft Storyboard

by Olivia Schmidt 1st October 2023 EME 6613

“Candidates lead their peers in designing and implementing technology-supported learning.”

For this deliverable, I took a leadership position in putting together our first storyboard for our online, interactive experience developed for the course Introduction to Hospitality and Tourism Management. In this storyboard, you will find details on the basic course flow and navigation of our experience, the route that students will take through Tampa on each of the stops, and a detailed description of each stop and what students will learn there. We also include details on how students will be assessed, and what the course will include in terms of elements - such as on-screen text, character dialogue, static imagery, animation, navigational elements, accessibility features, etc. My leadership in this assignment primarily encompassed putting together the presentation visually, as well as writing the majority of the content contained within the storyboard. As this was our draft storyboard, we would use this product to build out our final storyboard, which more accurately presented how the experience would end up looking in Articulate Storyline.
